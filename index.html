Simple Process

From Visitor to Lead in Seconds

Your AI receptionist handles the entire conversation — no human needed until a lead is qualified and ready to talk.

A visitor lands on your website or messages you on Facebook. Your AI greets them instantly — any time of day or night.

02

AI Qualifies & Books

The AI collects name, phone, email, and their specific needs. It books appointments directly to your calendar or gathers info for a quote.

03

You Get Alerted Instantly

The moment a new lead comes in, you get a text and email alert. Every lead is scored Hot, Warm, or Cold — so you know who to call first.
